G Sky Defense is a thrilling 2D game that allows the user to fend off invasions of fighter aircraft!
The fighter aircraft fly in at various speeds and altitudes, and at each level it gets harder to shoot aircraft out of the sky!
This game has 5 different levels with unique landscapes! Gain enough points to go to the next level, but don't run out of time!
Points are also lost when an aircraft is not shot down! Try out G Sky Defense today!
